# Extraction Priority Guide

Use the extraction priority matrix after the legibility audit.

## Axes

- Business criticality: how much the capability matters to revenue or compliance
- Tribal knowledge density: the inverted legibility score
- Agent touchpoint frequency: how often agents will need to navigate the capability

## Scoring

Multiply the three axes. Do not add them. Multiplication keeps zero-axis capabilities out of the top of the list.

## Phases

- Phase 1: score 9
- Phase 2: score 4 to 8
- Phase 3: score 1 to 3
- Score 0: not a current extraction candidate

## Output

Use the matrix to decide which capabilities to scaffold first with `cdad init`.
