# Extraction Priority Guide

Use this guide after a legibility audit when you need to decide what to extract first.

## Axes

- Business criticality captures how much revenue or compliance risk sits in the capability
- Tribal knowledge density is derived from the legibility audit score
- Agent touchpoint frequency captures how often agents will hit the capability

## Rule

Multiply the three axes. Do not add them. A zero on any axis should keep the capability out of the front of the line.

## Phases

- Phase 1 is score `9`
- Phase 2 is score `4` to `8`
- Phase 3 is score `1` to `3`
- Score `0` means the capability is not a current extraction candidate
